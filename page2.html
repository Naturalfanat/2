Interesting facts:

✩Indigenous Heritage:
Before European contact, Canada was home to over 600 distinct Indigenous nations, each with its own languages, cultures, and governance systems.

✩French and British Rivalry:
The struggle for control over Canada between France and Britain culminated in the Seven Years' War (1756-1763), which ended with France ceding most of its North American territories to Britain.

✩The Last Province:
Newfoundland was the last province to join Canada in 1949, becoming the 10th province.

✩Women’s Suffrage:
Canadian women gained the right to vote in federal elections in 1918, although some Indigenous women did not gain this right until 1960.
