✩Confederation:
In 1867, Canada became a self-governing Dominion through the British North America Act, initially uniting Ontario, Quebec, New Brunswick, and Nova Scotia

✩20th Century:
Canada participated in both World Wars, fostering a sense of national identity. In 1982, the Constitution Act was enacted, including the Charter of Rights and Freedoms
